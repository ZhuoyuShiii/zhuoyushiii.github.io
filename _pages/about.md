I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at University of Southern California, working with Prof. <a style="color:inherit" href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=u-8h3HcAAAAJ&hl=en">Fred Morstatter</a>. I use computational methods and social media data to understand social behavior, especially how people use and are influenced by causal language.
